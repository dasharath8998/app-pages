Welcome

Your personal recording studio

Video Prompt Studio combines a professional-grade teleprompter with a full-featured video recorder in a single, easy-to-use mobile app. Whether you're teaching an online course, filming a product review, or preparing a keynote presentation, the app lets you read your script naturally while the camera captures your performance — no memorisation, no repeated takes, no stress.

Everything runs on your device. Write your script, adjust the scroll speed to match your pace, choose your layout, press record, and deliver your best take — every time.

Our Mission

To make professional-quality video creation accessible to every creator — by bringing the teleprompter and the camera together in one simple, private, and powerful app.
