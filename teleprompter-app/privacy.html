Section 01

Introduction

Video Prompt Studio ("the app," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that combines a professional teleprompter with video recording capabilities. It is designed for content creators, educators, presenters, and anyone who wants to record polished videos while reading a scrolling script.

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Prompt Studio handles information when you use the app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The core principle of this policy is straightforward: we do not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal information.

By using Video Prompt Studio, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Section 02

Information We Collect

Short answer: We do not collect any personal information. Everything the app creates — scripts, videos, and settings — is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us or any third party.

Video Prompt Studio does not:

  • Require you to create an account or register
  • Collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information
  • Collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Record or transmit your location
  • Use analytics or crash-reporting services that send data off your device
  • Use advertising networks or collect advertising-related data
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Collect usage statistics or behavioral data
  • Perform background data collection of any kind
Section 03

How We Use Information

Because we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data for us to use. All content you create within the app — including scripts, video recordings, and customised settings — is used solely to provide you with the app's features and remains entirely on your device.

App preferences (such as font size, scroll speed, and theme selection) are stored in your device's local storage and are never transmitted anywhere.

Section 04

Permissions

Video Prompt Studio requests only the minimum permissions required for its core recording functionality. All recordings and scripts are saved to the app's private internal storage — no storage or media library permission is requested or needed on any platform.

📷 Camera

Required to display the camera preview and record video. The camera feed is processed entirely on your device. No video is transmitted to any server.

🎙️ Microphone

Required to capture audio as part of your video recordings. Audio is recorded and saved locally on your device and is never uploaded or shared.

🎤 Speech Recognition (optional)

Optionally requested to enable voice-assisted teleprompter scrolling. Speech recognition, when active, processes your voice locally to detect spoken words and advance the script position. This feature is entirely optional and can be disabled at any time.

The app does not request access to your photo library, media library, or external storage. All files it creates are stored in its own private app container and are accessible only to the app itself.

Section 05

Data Storage

All data generated by Video Prompt Studio is stored locally on your device using your operating system's standard local storage mechanisms, including:

  • Scripts — saved in the app's local database on your device
  • Video recordings — saved to your device's internal storage
  • App settings and preferences — saved in local shared preferences

No data is synced to cloud services, transmitted to our servers, or shared with any external party by the app itself. If your device operating system or a third-party app you have installed performs its own backups (for example, iCloud on iOS or Google Drive backup on Android), your locally stored data may be included in those device-level backups according to the policies of those services, which are outside our control.

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or using your device's app storage management settings.

Section 06

Data Security

Because Video Prompt Studio stores all data locally on your device, the security of your data is primarily determined by your device's own security features, such as device encryption, screen lock, and operating system protections.

We encourage you to use strong device security settings, including a screen lock and up-to-date operating system software, to protect your device and the data stored on it.

Since we do not receive or store your data on any server, there is no central database that could be compromised to expose your information.

Section 07

Third-Party Services

Video Prompt Studio is built using the Flutter framework and relies on standard operating system components (Android and iOS APIs) to provide camera access, audio recording, file management, and media playback. These are system-level capabilities provided by Google and Apple as part of their respective operating systems.

The app does not intentionally integrate third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers. It does not transmit user content — including scripts, recordings, or settings — to any external servers.

When the app displays text using Google Fonts loaded from the internet, your device may make a network request to Google's font servers to download the font files. This behaviour is subject to Google's own privacy policies. No personal data or app content is sent as part of these font requests.

The app does not contain any third-party advertising SDKs or monetisation frameworks.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Video Prompt Studio does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

Because the app collects no personal data whatsoever, it poses no elevated privacy risk to minors. However, as a general content-creation tool, the app is intended for use by individuals who are at least 13 years of age. Younger children should use the app only with parental supervision.

Section 09

Your Rights and Control

Because Video Prompt Studio does not collect or process your personal data on any server, there is no personal data held by us for you to access, correct, or request deletion of.

You retain full control over your data at all times:

  • Delete scripts — directly within the app using the delete option on any script
  • Delete recordings — directly within the app or from your device's file manager
  • Delete all app data — by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device's settings
  • Revoke permissions — at any time from your device's Settings > Apps > Video Prompt Studio > Permissions
