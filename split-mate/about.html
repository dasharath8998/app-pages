TripSplit is an offline-first expense sharing app. No login. No cloud. No tracking. Just friends, trips, and fair splits.

Our mission ↓
Mission

Why we built this

Splitting expenses with friends should be simple, private, and work anywhere — even when there's no signal.

Most expense-splitting apps require a phone number, email, or social login. They sync your data to a server, store it indefinitely, and monetise your behaviour. We disagreed with that model.

TripSplit stores everything locally on your device. When you need to sync with a friend, you share a QR code — nothing leaves your phone unless you choose to share it.

Privacy Commitment

Your data stays with you

We built TripSplit around one core belief: your financial data belongs to you alone. Unlike cloud-based apps, we cannot access, view, or monetise your expenses — because they never leave your device.
