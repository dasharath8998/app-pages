Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 July 2026  ·  Applies to: Status Saver (com.ds.statussaver)

This Privacy Policy explains how DS Consultant ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the Status Saver Android application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not have a backend server, does not require an account or sign-in, and does not send your photos, videos, or any other content anywhere. All browsing, saving, and sharing of statuses happens entirely and exclusively on your own device.

2. How the App accesses files

Status Saver uses Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF) — specifically ACTION_OPEN_DOCUMENT_TREE — to access files. This means:

  • You are shown the standard Android system folder picker and you choose exactly one folder to grant access to.
  • The App does not request the broad READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE or MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions.
  • The App can only read files inside the single folder you granted, and cannot browse the rest of your device's storage.
  • You can revoke this access at any time from the App's Settings screen or from your device's system settings.
  • Files you choose to download are saved locally to your device's Pictures/Status Saver or Movies/Status Saver folders using Android's MediaStore, so they appear in your normal gallery.

3. Permissions used

  • Internet — used only to open links you explicitly tap (such as opening a WhatsApp chat or a Play Store page). It is not used to collect analytics or transmit your data.
  • Storage Access Framework — as described above; no broad storage permission is requested.

4. Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not display ads. When you use the Direct Chat or Share features, the App opens WhatsApp or the standard Android share sheet — those destinations are governed by their own respective privacy policies, not this one.

5. Data sharing

Because we do not collect any personal data, we have no personal data to share, sell, or disclose to any third party.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Data retention & your choices

Since no data is collected by us, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on your behalf. Any files you choose to download remain on your device under your control, and you may delete them at any time from within the App or your device's file manager. You may also revoke the App's folder access permission at any time.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
