About Status Saver

Last updated: 24 July 2026

Status Saver is a simple, modern utility that helps you browse, save, and share statuses from the WhatsApp Statuses folder that you choose on your own device. It's built with a clean, premium interface and works entirely on-device — nothing you view or save is ever uploaded anywhere.

The app is developed and maintained by DS Consultant, an independent developer focused on building small, focused, privacy-respecting utilities.

What the app does

  • Browse image and video statuses from a WhatsApp folder you explicitly grant access to
  • Save a copy of a status to your device's gallery
  • Share a saved status to WhatsApp or any other app
  • Open a WhatsApp chat with any number directly, without saving it as a contact
